## Public Artist Bio (Outer World)

Samzin Kreave is a multidisciplinary creator whose work lives at the intersection of music, story, and systems. Known for building emotionally resonant worlds rather than isolated releases, Samzin approaches creation as architecture — showing how sound, narrative, and identity interact rather than compete.

Born April 10, 1988, Samzin’s work reflects a phase of creative integration where experience sharpens expression instead of dulling it. His music carries the weight of lived emotion without posturing, while his storytelling explores consent, control, memory, and the cost of survival inside broken systems.

Rather than chasing trends, Samzin Kreave focuses on resonance — creating work that listeners don’t just consume, but recognize. His projects under the SoulSoundWorld umbrella treat art as a shared space: something entered, not sold.

Samzin Kreave doesn’t aim to dominate attention.  
He builds worlds that stay.
