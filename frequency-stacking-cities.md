Frequency Stacking — Cities, Capitals, Threats & Safe Havens
Worlds are not maps.
They are pressures given shape.
Cities are where those pressures become visible.
Capital Cities
1. Velocity Prime — Capital of Acceleration
Theme: Speed, optimization, productivity
A city where everything updates itself.
Buildings reconfigure
Jobs change mid-shift
Relationships dissolve without conflict
Dominant Pressures:
Time Acceleration
Emotional Extraction
Identity Drift
People do not collapse here. They fade.
2. Archive Null — Capital of Memory
Theme: Preservation, record, truth
A city made of libraries, data vaults, and living archives.
Dominant Pressures:
Narrative Distortion
Memory Overwrite
Identity Fragmentation
People do not die here. They become footnotes.
3. The Fracture Loop — Capital of Collapse
Theme: Instability, repetition, loss
A looping city that resets every collapse cycle.
Dominant Pressures:
Time Loops
Scarcity
Meaning Inflation
People do not escape here. They repeat.

Safe Havens
Safe havens do not remove pressure.
They metabolize it.

A slow city where nothing is optimized.
Time passes normally here.
The Listening House
A place where stories are spoken aloud until they settle.
The Fold
A mobile refuge that appears where coherence is highest.
It is never in the same place twice.
